Scrubs Wiki

Scrubs was an american television show that ran from 2001-to-2010. It had a total of nine seasons and was shot in the greater California area. It was a comedy show that centered on the life of J.D, a resident medical student at a hospital, and the absurd and often slapstick adventures he and his fellow doctors have in the hospital.
